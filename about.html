About Kolkata Knight Riders

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) is a premier franchise cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing the vibrant city of Kolkata, West Bengal. Founded in 2008, KKR quickly emerged as one of the most popular and successful teams in the IPL, capturing the hearts of millions of fans not just in India but across the globe. The franchise is co-owned by Bollywood icon Shah Rukh Khan, celebrated actress Juhi Chawla, and her spouse Jay Mehta, which has contributed significantly to its widespread popularity and media attention.

KKR is known for its distinct purple and gold colors, symbolizing royalty and pride, and its iconic home ground, the Eden Gardens. With a seating capacity of over 66,000, Eden Gardens is one of the oldest and most revered cricket stadiums globally, offering an electric atmosphere during KKR's home matches. The passionate fan base, often referred to as the "KKR Army," is known for their unwavering support, making Eden Gardens a fortress for the team.

Over the years, Kolkata Knight Riders have established themselves as a formidable force in the IPL. The team has clinched the IPL title twice, first in 2012 and again in 2014, under the astute leadership of Gautam Gambhir. These victories are etched in the memories of cricket enthusiasts as they marked KKR's rise to prominence in one of the most competitive T20 leagues in the world.

KKR has been home to several cricketing greats and dynamic players who have left an indelible mark on the game. The team has featured legendary cricketers like Sourav Ganguly, who was the face of the franchise in its early years, as well as powerhouses like Sunil Narine, Andre Russell, and Brendon McCullum, who have thrilled fans with their match-winning performances.
